# THCA E-commerce Compliance Research Plan

## Task Overview
Research comprehensive compliance requirements for selling hemp-derived THCA products online, covering legal, regulatory, and operational aspects.

## Research Focus Areas
1. Age verification requirements for hemp-derived THCA products (21+ laws)
2. State-by-state hemp/THCA restrictions and legal considerations
3. Required legal disclaimers for THCA products
4. Lab testing and Certificate of Analysis (COA) display requirements
5. Payment processor compliance for hemp/THCA sales
6. Marketing compliance and FDA guidelines for hemp-derived products
