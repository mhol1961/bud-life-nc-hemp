# Buddy Brand AI Features Research Report

**Research Date:** August 18, 2025  
**Website Analyzed:** https://www.buddybrand.us  
**Objective:** Explore and identify AI chatbots, product recommendation engines, calculators, review systems, and engagement features

## Executive Summary

Buddy Brand operates a cannabis-focused mobile application with sophisticated AI-powered features designed to personalize the cannabis experience for users. While the website serves primarily as a marketing and information portal, the core AI functionality is implemented in their mobile app. The research revealed several advanced AI features including image recognition, personalized recommendation systems, and an AI chatbot assistant.

## Key AI Features Identified

### 1. AI-Powered Product Identification System
- **Technology:** Image recognition with machine learning
- **Functionality:** Users can photograph any cannabis product and receive instant identification and comprehensive product information
- **Processing Speed:** Information retrieved "in seconds"
- **Data Source:** Integrated with their "knowledge machine" database

### 2. Personalized Recommendation Engine
- **Feature Name:** "Close Match To You" system
- **Functionality:** Calculates percentage-based compatibility scores between products and user preferences
- **Example:** Shows "34% match" with explanation "This product is close to what you usually like but not perfect"
- **Personalization:** Tailors recommendations based on individual user data and preferences

### 3. AI Chatbot Assistant ("Ask Buddy")
- **Interface:** Interactive chatbot accessible via "Ask Buddy About This" buttons
- **Functionality:** Provides conversational AI assistance for product inquiries, effects information, and personalized guidance
- **Integration:** Available throughout the product experience for contextual help

### 4. Knowledge Machine
- **Purpose:** AI-enhanced information retrieval and data management system
- **Capabilities:** Provides meaningful and trusted cannabis information
- **Integration:** Powers the instant product identification feature

### 5. Social Review System with AI Analysis
- **Community Features:** Real user reviews and experience sharing
- **Trust Verification:** System for genuine opinion verification
- **Social Networking:** Friend connections and shared experiences
