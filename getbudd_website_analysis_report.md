## Executive Summary

During the analysis of getbudd.com, I encountered multiple redirections to different websites, indicating that the original domain may have undergone changes in ownership or purpose. The research revealed three distinct websites that getbudd.com redirected to during the session:

1. **Primary Site Found:** BUDD CBD Products (https://www.getbudd.com/)
