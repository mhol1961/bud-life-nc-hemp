# Bud Life NC Website Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

This report documents attempts to analyze the product catalog on budlifenc.com as requested. During the research process, multiple navigation attempts revealed that budlifenc.com consistently redirects to other cannabis-related websites (primarily getbudd.com and buddybrand.us). When attempting to access the product catalog directly via budlifenc.com/shop, a 404 error page was encountered, indicating that the requested page does not exist.

## Research Methodology

1. **Direct Navigation Attempts**:
   - Multiple attempts to navigate to https://budlifenc.com resulted in redirects to getbudd.com and buddybrand.us
   - Screenshots were captured to document the redirects
   - Interactive elements were analyzed on the redirected pages

2. **Alternative Access Attempts**:
   - Direct navigation to the shop page (https://www.budlifenc.com/shop) was attempted
   - This resulted in a 404 error page, confirming the page does not exist

## Findings

### Redirect Behavior

When attempting to access budlifenc.com, the browser was consistently redirected to:
1. **getbudd.com** - A CBD product website with various CBD products
2. **buddybrand.us** - A cannabis products website featuring various cannabis-related items

### Content Access Issues

- The requested product catalog was not accessible via the budlifenc.com domain
- The 404 error on budlifenc.com/shop confirms that the specific product catalog section requested does not exist
- Multiple attempts produced the same results, suggesting this is not a temporary issue

The following screenshots document the redirect behavior and 404 error:
1. getbudd_homepage.png - Shows the getbudd.com homepage after redirect from budlifenc.com
2. buddybrand_products.png - Shows the buddybrand.us products page after redirect from budlifenc.com

## Conclusions

Based on the research conducted, it appears that budlifenc.com is no longer operational in its original form. The consistent redirects to other cannabis-related websites suggest that the domain may have been acquired by or redirected to these other brands. The 404 error on the /shop path further confirms that the product catalog requested for analysis is not accessible.

## Recommendations

1. **Verify Domain Status**: Consider verifying the current status of budlifenc.com through domain registration records
2. **Alternative Research**: If information about Bud Life NC products is still needed, consider researching through:
   - Social media channels that might reference the brand
   - Business directory listings that might have updated information
   - Direct contact with the company through other means if available
3. **Analyze Redirect Destinations**: If information about the current website owners is desired, consider analyzing getbudd.com and buddybrand.us further
