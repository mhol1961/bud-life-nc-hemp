**Date:** August 18, 2025

**Status:** Site Redirect Investigation and Alternative Cannabis Site Analysis

## Executive Summary

The original budlifenc.com website appears to have undergone significant changes, with the domain now redirecting to multiple different cannabis/hemp sites including getbudd.com, buddybrand.us, and binoidcbd.com. This research documents the redirect behavior and analyzes the cannabis product offerings found on the alternative sites that are accessible through these redirects.

### 1. Domain Redirect Issue

- **Original Request:** budlifenc.com
